Our Story

Clean and Service is a cleaning service company that was founded in 2013 in Dhaka, Bangladesh. We began with a small team and a big dream: to provide best cleaning services to homes and businesses in the Dhaka city. Over the years, we have grown significantly, expanding our services and building a reputation for excellence.
